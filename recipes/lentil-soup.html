Lentil Soup

Description

Best and quickest lentil soup ever! A friend of mine gave me this recipe and it's now on of my rotation meals.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • 6 garlic cloves roughly chopped
  • 1 medium onion minced
  • 1 large carrot chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. vegetable stock paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups of cooked lentils
  • 6 cups of hot water

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat the olive oil and add the onions, garlic and carrot. Cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the bay leaves, tomato paste, vegetable stock paste, salt and pepper. Stir very well.
  3. Turn the heat to high and add the lentils and water and cook for 20 min, or until the carrots are tender.
  4. Done! You can serve with vinegar if you like it.
