Crepes

Description

Delicious crepes recipe that my mother passed on to me!

Ingredients

  • 300g of flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 or 3 spoons of oil or butter
  • 700ml of milk (any milk of choice is fine)
  • 3 spoons of sugar
  • 8g of vanilla sugar or just a splash of vanilla extract
  • a pinch of salt
  • The secret ingredient: a bit of Porto wine!

Steps

(Get ready, this has many steps)

  1. Mix everything with the blender
  2. Put a bit of butter or oil in the frying pan
  3. Fry them in the pan - done!
