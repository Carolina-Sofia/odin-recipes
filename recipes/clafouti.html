Clafouti

Description

French dessert that is perfect for summer! This dessert uses 500g of fruit. Some of the best for this are:

  • Cherry
  • Blueberry
  • Any mix of berries

Ingredients

  • 80g of flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 30g of melted butter
  • 250ml of milk (any milk of choice is fine)
  • 110g of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking power
  • a pinch of salt
  • 500g of fruit

Steps

(Get ready, this has many steps)

  1. Mix everything with the blender (except the fruit)
  2. Put a bit of butter in the pan
  3. First put the fruit in the pan, followed by the dough
  4. Bake in the oven at 180ยบ for about 20 min or until it is gold on top
